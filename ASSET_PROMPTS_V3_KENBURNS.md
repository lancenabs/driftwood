# ASSET PROMPTS v3 — THE 30 PICTURES THAT SLOW AND ZOOM
### Driftwood · the heartfelt transitions (Lance's 2026-07-12 commission: "at least 30 pictures that slow and zoom, for heart felt transitions throughout")

**Format for every image:** 1920×1080 landscape (the Ken Burns pan needs margin), painterly storybook realism, warm golden-amber palette against teal sea, soft rim light. **One shared style reference across the whole set** (the Foundry discipline). No text in image. Each entry lists WHERE it plays so wiring them is mechanical.

**The robots:** small, hand-sized to knee-height, built of weathered driftwood planks and branch-limbs, round knot-of-pine eyes, visible rope lashings and copper nails. Skip is the smallest — mismatched scraps, one hand wrapped in a palm-leaf bandage (after image 7).

## ACT I · THE WRECK (cold open + Season 1) — plays: boarding cinematic + S1 milestone opens

1. **THE DOCK** — A family of four on a weathered pier at golden hour, mid-argument, faces turned away from each other; behind them a small tour boat and a broad-shouldered old guide (Mr. Bauer, canvas coat, white beard, kind heavy eyes) watching them with knowing sadness. *(boarding beat 1)*
2. **BAUER AT THE WHEEL** — Close on Mr. Bauer's weathered hands on a boat wheel, a brass compass hanging from his wrist on a leather cord, horizon behind. *(boarding beat 3)*
3. **THE STORM WALL** — The little boat tiny against a towering black-green storm front swallowing the sky, one shaft of gold still on the water. *(boarding beat 4)*
4. **HOLD THE LINE** — Inside the storm: four pairs of hands gripping one rope, spray and dark, faces lit by a single lantern. *(boarding beat 5 — his last order)*
5. **THE TIDE LINE** — Dawn. Four figures collapsed on wet sand among planks and rope, alive, holding each other; the sea flat and innocent behind. *(boarding beat 7)*
6. **THE COMPASS ASHORE** — Macro: the brass compass half-buried in sand, engraved "TO M.B. — COME HOME", needle pointing inland toward jungle. *(boarding final beat)*
7. **SKIP** — First full portrait: the smallest driftwood robot standing in front of a jumble of beach wood, holding a small crab like a shield, terrified and adorable. *(milestone 1 open)*
8. **THE BURNED HAND** — Night. Firelight. The family's hands together smothering the flame on Skip's small wooden fist with wet sand — fear and tenderness in every face. *(milestone 3)*
9. **THE CHARCOAL CONTRACT** — Skip writing laboriously on a plank with charcoal, tongue out; "CHALLENGE LOG — THEM" legible as marks, family watching in firelight. *(milestone 3 close)*
10. **FIRST NIGHT WATCH** — The family asleep in a huddle; Skip sitting on a rock keeping watch over them, moonlit sea. *(milestone 6 / S1 curtain)*

## ACT II · THE VILLAGE (Season 2) — plays: S2 milestone opens + curtain

11. **THE JUMBLE VILLAGE REVEALED** — A hidden village of driftwood huts in jungle clearing: rope bridges, shell wind-chimes, a dozen small wooden robots pausing mid-chore to stare. *(milestone 7)*
12. **THE COLLIER** — The oldest, largest robot at a forge of coral-stone, hammer in hand, sparks rising; scars of many repairs, patient eyes. *(milestone 8)*
13. **THE RULES POST** — A carved totem-post of house rules in pictograms, robots' handiwork; the youngest child tracing one with a finger. *(milestone 9)*
14. **THE SLEEPING SPIRAL** — Overhead shot: the whole Jumble asleep in a spiral formation, smallest in the center — Skip's spot conspicuously empty because he's sleeping at the family's camp. *(milestone 10)*
15. **THE FIVE OILS** — A robot shelf of five small clay jars, each glowing faint gold, labeled in pictograms (touch, words, time, gifts, deeds). *(milestone 11)*
16. **BAUER'S KNIFE** — A weathered clasp-knife with "M.B." on the handle, lying on moss at an old cold campsite, years of patina. *(milestone 12 — the red thread)*
17. **THE REMEMBERING HOUSE** — Interior: walls carved floor-to-ceiling with reliefs of a bearded man building robots from storm-wreck; lantern light; the family staring up. *(milestone 13 / S2 curtain)*

## ACT III · THE HEARTH (Season 3) — plays: S3 milestone opens + curtain

19. **GAME NIGHT** — The driftwood game box open on a mat by the fire, shells and carved pieces, robot and human hands reaching in together. *(milestone 15)*

## ACT IV · THE STORM RETURNS (Season 4) — plays: S4 milestone opens + curtain

23. **THE SECOND STORM** — The camp in ruin at grey dawn, roof gone, the family standing in the wreckage — but this time facing INTO each other, not away. *(milestone 20)*
24. **NAMING THE UNDERTOW** — The family and Hollow at the tide line; beneath the clear shallows a dark current visibly pulling seaward — the villain made visible, nothing more than water. *(milestone 20 close)*
25. **THE REPAIR ROPE** — Macro: old rope of a dozen splices in different lashings, two human hands and one wooden hand tying the newest splice together. *(milestone 23)*
26. **THE SEARCH LINE** — Night, rain: the family + robots hand-in-hand in one linked line crossing dark jungle, lanterns swinging, fierce faces. *(milestone 25 — the missing child)*
27. **THE GLOW BEHIND THE FALLS** — The waterfall at night from outside: warm firelight glowing THROUGH the falling water, small silhouette of the youngest safe inside. *(milestone 25 final beat)*
28. **BAUER'S WORKSHOP** — Inside the cave: workbench, tools worn to hand-shapes, shelves of practice robot limbs, the burned-in plank reading as carved marks, an old photograph of four sun-squinting faces. *(milestone 26 — the reveal)*

## ACT V · THE LAUNCH (Season 5) — plays: S5 milestone opens + finale

29. **EVERY PLANK HAS A NAME** — The half-built raft on the beach at golden hour, each plank with charcoal writing on it; family and Jumble building side by side. *(milestone 27)*
30. **THE TOTEM** — Lantern-ring night ceremony at the tall carved village totem; the Collier's chisel mid-cut on fresh names; "vouching" solemnity; Skip standing on a stump. *(milestone 30)*
31. **THE COMPASS IN THEIR HANDS** — Macro, dawn light: Skip's small wooden hands pressing the polished compass into the family's cupped hands; the needle pointing at THEM. *(milestone 31 — the last image before rescue)*
32. **THE FIRE LIT** — Wide: the cliff-top signal fire finally blazing tall at dawn, smoke straight up, the family and the whole Jumble silhouetted; a boat shape on the horizon. *(finale / credits)*

## WIRING (for the build session after the art lands)
- Files land in `public/story/act{1-5}/NN_slug.jpg` (e.g. `act1/06_compass_ashore.jpg`).
- One shared CSS class does the Ken Burns: `animation: kenburns 8s ease-in-out both` — `@keyframes kenburns { from { transform: scale(1.06) translate(1.5%, 1%); } to { transform: scale(1.18) translate(-1.5%, -1%); } }` with a 1.2s cross-fade between beats.
- The `Beats` renderer (MilestoneLog.tsx) gains an optional `art?: string` field per beat — when present, the image renders as a full-bleed panel above the text with the slow zoom; missing files fall back to text-only (the auto-light law, honest both ways).
- The boarding cinematic (BoardingStory.tsx CINEMATIC array) gains the same `art` slots for images 1–6.
- Season curtains already accept `/shore/curtain{N}.mp4` — stills 10, 17, 22, 28, 32 double as poster frames.
